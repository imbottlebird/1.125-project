WHY DO WE CARE?

Sentiment analysis can help understand how positive or negative the reviews are in the context of dining and restaurants. Positive words for example maybe ‘awesome’, ‘great’, ‘amazing’ etc. while the negative ones maybe ‘unprofessional’, ‘yuk’, ‘refund’, ‘bad’ etc.

  • We are trying to help identify the emotional state a restaurant has with its diners through this. We can check if the attitude of the diners is negative/positive/neutral.
  • A sentiment analysis of this kind may help the diners make informed decisions about where to eat and what to eat, and also give the restaurants a chance to know real time from diners as to how they are performing.
